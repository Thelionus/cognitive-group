The Pedigree

Born from Deep Research

The Cognitive Group, headquartered in Montreal, was founded in 1995 as a specialized spinoff of the Computer Research Institute of Montreal (CRIM).

Building on decades of expertise in Cognitive Ergonomics, we partnered with Royal Bank of Canada (RBC) for over 10 years to optimize high-stakes loan origination. This institutional rigor is the DNA of our deterministic AI engine.

Strategic Scale

Our alliance with CGI allows us to combine the agility of a deep-tech lab with the global delivery assurance required by Tier-1 institutions.
