Impact Finance

Scaling Financial Inclusion with AI.

How Impact Labs used Cognitive Agents to reduce loan origination time from 6 hours to 15 minutes, rescuing clients from predatory lenders.

96% Faster Origination
100% Custom "Shadow Core"
Zero Predatory Rates

The Problem: The Vicious Circle

Impact Labs, a division of a leading financial institution, serves clients trapped in a vicious circle. Due to life events like illness, divorce, or job loss, these individuals have poor credit scores. Rejected by traditional banks, they turn to predatory lenders charging exorbitant interest rates, making repayment impossible.

Impact Labs pays off these predatory loans and refinances the client at a reasonable rate (10-30%). However, this market is "underserved" for a reason:

  • Traditional Scoring Fails: Their credit scores are already ruined, so automated bank algorithms reject them instantly.
  • Painstaking Manual Review: Assessing risk requires deep forensic analysis (pay stubs, bank statements, character assessment).
  • Economic Unviability: It took 5 to 6 hours to process a single low-value loan.

The Solution: A "Shadow Core" & Behavioral AI

To make financial inclusion scalable, we needed to automate the un-automatable. We deployed the Cognitive AI Workbench combined with BusinessBANKER.

1. The "Shadow" Core Banking System

Deploying a traditional Core Banking System (like Temenos or SAP) was too expensive for this specific portfolio. Instead, we built a lightweight, API-driven "Shadow Core" specifically for loan management, integrated directly into the AI workflow.

2. The Pre-Trained AI Agents

We replaced manual forensic work with specialized AI agents pre-trained on banking logic:

  • Cross-Check Agent: Validates identity by cross-referencing Equifax data with pay stubs (ignoring the bad score).
  • Income Analyzer: Deciphers highly variable pay stub formats and frequencies that confuse standard OCR.
  • Behavioral Analysis (The "Character" Test): Using Cognitive Task Analysis, we encoded expert intuition to assess "Character"—is the client punctual? Is their story consistent? Are their bank statement behaviors aligned with their claims?

The Result: From Paperwork to People

The transformation was drastic. The time to prepare and adjudicate a loan dropped from 6 hours to 15 minutes.

More importantly, this efficiency changed the role of the staff. Instead of spending their day doing data entry and forensic document checking, loan officers now focus on sociological consulting—listening to the client's story, understanding their life events, and providing financial coaching.

Impact Labs successfully automated a complex, high-risk workflow to deliver financial relief to those who need it most.
