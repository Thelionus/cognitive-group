01 | The Challenge

The Brain Drain & Verification Bottleneck

We collaborated with a major Canadian financial institution employing 300 adjudicators. Our initial analysis revealed that adjudicators spent **90% of their time on routine verification** and only 10% on actual decision-making.

Simultaneously, the bank faced a "Brain Drain." The intuition required for complex risk assessment resided in senior adjudicators nearing retirement. This expertise was undocumented, leaving the institution vulnerable.

"I have to flip back and forth between five different systems just to verify one income statement. It's inconsistent and exhausting."
— Senior Credit Officer
02 | The Solution

Industrializing Expert Intuition

Using **Cognitive Task Analysis (CTA)**, we extracted the "Gold Standard" logic from the bank's top experts. We mapped 100 years of collective lending expertise into a deterministic code structure.

01
PROTOCOL CAPTURE: Mapping senior adjudicator mental models.
02
LOGIC SYNTHESIS: Converting intuition into auditable rules.
03
DEPLOYMENT: Pre-trained Agents automate 90% of verification.

By automating the capture and verification of identity, income, and property valuation, we ensured that the application of bank policy is consistent, traceable, and instant.

03 | The Impact

Deterministic ROI

The implementation fundamentally shifted the operating model of the lending unit:

  • Expertise Encapsulated: The bank now owns a digital asset containing its best decision-making logic, immune to staff turnover.
  • OpEx Reduction: Estimated **$20 million** in operational savings through streamlined workflows.
  • Risk Mitigation: 100% adherence to complex environmental and regulatory questionnaires.
