Strategy Session
Conversational Banking

Credit Origination via WhatsApp.

Moving financial services from complex web forms to natural language conversations. We enabled a fully automated credit channel accessible to 2 billion people.

Zero User Friction
Global Accessibility
100% Auto-Structured Data

The Challenge: The "Form" Barrier

Banks have traditionally relied on web portals and call centers. However, in mobile-first regions (Africa, South America, parts of Europe), complex web forms have low conversion rates. They require high digital literacy and reliable broadband.

The Opportunity: For 2 billion people, the internet is WhatsApp. The goal was to move financial services to where the customers actually live—inside their chat apps.

The Solution: Cognitive Interface

We didn't build a chatbot that forces users to select options from a menu (e.g., "Press 1 for Loans"). We deployed a Natural-Language Cognitive Interface. It allows customers to speak freely, while the AI Workbench structures their intent in real-time.

How It Works

The Technical Workflow

  • Natural Language Interaction: The system accepts free-form language, informal phrasing, and mixed formats (e.g., "100k", "100 000").
  • Dynamic Interviewing: The AI asks only what is needed based on the conversation context, mirroring a human account manager.
  • Real-Time Structuring: As the user chats, the AI Workbench populates a structured loan request inside Business Banker. No re-keying required.
  • Document Capture: Users send photos/PDFs via chat. The system automatically captures, validates, and links them to the loan record.

Strategic Impact

This is not a channel experiment; it is a paradigm shift. By embedding cognitive reasoning into conversational interfaces, the bank extended its reach to populations previously excluded by digital complexity. Analysts now focus on judgment, while the AI handles the interview.
