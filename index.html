Three-Pillar Architecture

Cognitive Machines is a unified, AI-orchestrated platform that replaces fragmented legacy stacks with a single, intelligent system.

01

Cognitive Core

Modular deposit and account engine. Handles the foundational banking operations with 90% less complexity than traditional cores.

  • Modular account and ledger management
  • Real-time transaction processing
  • Multi-currency and multi-entity support
  • API-first design for seamless integration
  • Regulatory compliance built-in (GDPR, SOC 2)
02

Business Banker

No-code loan origination and workflow engine. Deploy complex lending products in hours, not months. Zero manual configuration required.

  • Visual workflow designer for any loan type
  • Automated document processing and verification
  • Intelligent risk scoring and decisioning
  • Built-in compliance and audit trails
  • Multi-channel origination (web, mobile, branch)
03

AI Workbench

Orchestrates specialized cognitive agents across your entire banking operation. Automates everything from KYC to customer service to fraud detection.

  • Self-generating agents for new workflows
  • Natural language interfaces for staff
  • Predictive analytics and anomaly detection
  • Automated compliance monitoring
  • Continuous learning from institutional data

About Cognitive Group

Founded in 1995 by the Montreal Computer Research Institute (CRIM), Cognitive Group has pioneered the integration of cognitive psychology and engineering to deliver unmatched simplicity of systems, solutions, and processes. Through our unique expertise in Cognitive Engineering, we help the world's most demanding companies reduce costs and accelerate time to market.

Our Mission

To design solutions that make people feel accomplished and productive. We believe that if we put people first, the results will follow.

Our Approach: We understand sophisticated processes through cognitive engineering, then translate these discoveries into innovative technologies and processes that people actually want to use.

Timeline of Innovation

  • 1995: Founded as cognitive engineering practice
  • 2008: 10-year collaboration with RBC begins
  • 2017: BusinessBANKER deployed at BNDE
  • 2018: Expansion to GBDK (Lisbon)
  • 2020: BNDE becomes first African bank to offer COVID-19 loans
  • Today: Operating across North America, Africa, Asia, and beyond

Our Philosophy & Approach

People First

At the heart of every organization are people. Even in the most automated processes, people design, supervise, and maintain systems. Our mission is to design solutions that make people feel accomplished and productive.

Cognitive engineering integrates cognitive psychology to design and develop engineering systems intended to support users' cognitive processes. It encompasses human-machine interaction and contributes to the design of the entire system.

"Put people first and results will follow."

What We Deliver

Cognitive Analysis

Deep understanding of human behavior and complex processes. We analyze how people actually work, not how they're supposed to work.

Process Optimization

Eliminate unnecessary complexity. Reduce steps, time, and costs while improving quality and user experience.

AI Integration

Leverage cognitive engineering principles with modern AI. Automate what machines do best while keeping humans in control.

Implementation
