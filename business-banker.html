Commercial Loan Origination System

Cognitive Engine

Faster Decisions. Lower Cost. Stronger Governance.

A next-generation Commercial Loan Origination System (CLOS) designed for financial institutions seeking to accelerate lending, reduce operational friction, and embed expert credit judgment directly into automated workflows.

Core Capabilities

Extreme Configurability

Fully no-code configuration of products, workflows, and risk models. Credit policies can be adapted in real time without development cycles.

Integrated AI

Native automation for document analysis and financial spreading. Cognitive engines replicate expert reasoning with full traceability.

Speed to Market

Implementation in 6–8 weeks (vs. 6–12 months for traditional LOS) using iterative configuration and stakeholder validation.

Regulatory Alignment

Risk models aligned with Basel III/IV standards, supporting PD, LGD, EAD, and RWA calculations across jurisdictions.

Secure Cloud Architecture

Cognitive Engine is built on a robust, auto-scaling AWS infrastructure designed for high availability.

  • ✓ VPC Isolation: Private subnets for data.
  • ✓ Auto-Scaling: ECS Clusters adapt to load.
  • ✓ Resilience: Multi-AZ RDS deployment.
  • ✓ Defense: WAF Rules & Route 53.

Standard Decision Model

1. Origination

Account managers initiate onboarding, structure facilities, and draft term sheets. Client prospects are profiled with full entity mapping.

2. Adjudication

Credit analysts perform financial spreading and risk assessment. AI assists with anomaly detection and pre-computed risk ratios.

3. Operations

Legal and operations teams finalize documentation, ensure regulatory compliance, and manage disbursement.

AI-Driven Automation

Doc Intelligence Automated extraction/classification of data from PDFs.
Financial Spreading Normalization of unstructured financial statements.
Predictive Insights Pre-computed ratios, anomaly detection, and KPI suggestions.
