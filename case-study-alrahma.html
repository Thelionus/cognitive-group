01 | The Problem

The "Data Darkness" of the Informal Sector

Providing financing to small businesses in emerging economies is an information problem. In Senegal and Guinea, 80% of the population operates without bank accounts. Small businesses—market stalls, tailors, agricultural traders—do not possess financial statements, tax returns, or formal ledgers.

For decades, microfinance institutions relied on the "Gut Feeling" of loan officers. Methods were loosely structured, varying wildly from person to person. This lack of standardization made scaling impossible and risk assessment a game of chance.

02 | The Approach

Cognitive Task Analysis (CTA)

We did not just build an app. We deployed our Cognitive Task Analysis engine that comprise pre-trained micro-lenders. We answered: "How do you know a borrower is solvent when there is no paper trail?"

We discovered that experts use heuristic proxies. We codified these proxies into the BusinessBANKER™ mobile platform.

"The solution transforms the credit industry. We created a simple way to generate quantitative financial information from a qualitative assessment."

The "Grid Array" Protocol

Instead of asking for a Balance Sheet, the system prompts the Field Agent to collect "Proxy Data" using a structured grid. The AI then triangulates this into a financial profile.

INPUT (Field Agent) AI CALCULATION
> Cash on "Bad Day" > Lower Bound Revenue
> Cash on "Good Day" > Upper Bound Revenue
> Visual Stock Photo > Inventory Validation
> GPS Location > Foot Traffic Est.
STATUS: VALIDATED OUTPUT: P&L STATEMENT

This allows a loan officer to visit a client, take pictures, input answers to simple questions (e.g., "What are your sales on a bad day?"), and have the system auto-generate a P&L statement in real-time.

03 | The Results

Hyper-Efficiency in the Field

The formalization of this informal process fundamentally changed the unit economics of the bank.

  • Speed: Loan application creation dropped from hours of manual writing to 10 minutes on a tablet.
  • Cycle Time: The time from request to provision dropped to less than a few hours.
  • Focus: Loan officers stopped being data-entry clerks and returned to being relationship managers.
  • Agility: Using our No-Code configuration, Alrahma was able to adjust workflows (e.g., adding a Decision Committee step) in real-time without needing engineering support.
