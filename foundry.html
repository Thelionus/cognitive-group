The Methodology

From "Think Aloud" to
Deterministic Agents

STEP 01

Active Reasoning Capture

The system captures senior credit officers as they work. It stimulates and prompts officers to "think aloud" using cues based on time or user actions, capturing verbal reasoning, not just data inputs.

STEP 02

Cognitive Mapping

GenAI structures this unstructured reasoning into a Human Information Processing Map (Cognitive Task Analysis).

STEP 03

Deterministic Conversion

The logic map is converted into deterministic rules. The "Black Box" becomes a "Glass Box."

STEP 04

Agent Deployment

We deploy autonomous agents that execute this specific logic profile. No hallucinations. 100% Auditability.
