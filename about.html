History

Born from Research

The Cognitive Group (CG), headquartered in Montreal, was founded in 1995 by the Montreal Computer Research Institute (CRIM) to develop a Cognitive Engineering consulting practice.

Through our unique background in Cognitive Engineering and deep understanding of human behavior, we deliver unmatched simplicity. We believe that if we put people forward, the results will follow.

Partners

Strategic Ecosystem

Cognitive Group has forged partnerships with Maestria, Cofomo, and Central 1. These alliances directly benefit our clients, multiplying our capacity to fulfill needs with a group of more than 2,000 experts.
