Commercial Loan Origination Platform

Cognitive Engine

Faster Decisions. Lower Cost. Stronger Governance.

A next-generation Commercial Loan Origination System (CLOS) designed for financial institutions seeking to accelerate lending, reduce operational friction, and embed expert credit judgment directly into automated workflows.

Overview

Built on AI and cognitive engineering principles, Cognitive Engine enables banks to configure, deploy, and evolve complex lending processes without heavy IT projects. The platform scales seamlessly from micro-loans to large corporate and syndicated facilities, all within a single, unified system.

Cognitive Engine replaces fragmented tools and manual hand-offs with a single, intelligent platform that aligns origination, risk, and operations.

Core Capabilities & Business Impact

Extreme Configurability

Fully no-code configuration of products, workflows, and risk models. Adapt in real-time without dev cycles.

Scalable Architecture

Single-instance platform supporting personal, small business, and complex corporate multi-facility structures.

Integrated AI

Native automation for financial spreading and credit assessment with full traceability and reasoning.

Accelerated Deployment

Implementation in 6–8 weeks using iterative configuration and stakeholder validation.

Platform Architecture

1. Configurable Client Model

Dynamically constructed profiles support complex relationships, including:

  • Legal and trade names (Entity mapping)
  • Structure: Partnerships, Corporations, Individuals
  • Unified multi-entity, multi-product credit views

2. Loan Request Structure

The core object of Cognitive Engine, encompassing:

  • Facilities: Term loans, Revolving credit, Equipment, Mortgages, Leases.
  • Collateral: Security details with associated documentation and realizable values.
  • Conditions: Disbursement triggers and ongoing covenants (financial updates, annual reviews).

Workflow & Decision Model

Supporting the standard three-tier commercial lending structure used by Tier-1 banks:

01. Origination

Account managers initiate onboarding and structure facilities.

02. Adjudication

Analysts perform spreading and risk approvals with AI assistance.

03. Operations

Legal and Ops finalize documentation and disbursement.

Risk Modeling & Compliance

Configurable frameworks for small business, corporate, and construction financing. Supports:

• Internal rating methodologies & Basel-compliant metrics (PD, LGD, EAD, RWA).

• AI-assisted risk scoring and real-time portfolio visibility.

• Regulatory alignment with Basel III/IV standards across jurisdictions.

AI-Driven Automation

Natively embedded to enhance efficiency without compromising control:

Document Intelligence

Extraction and classification of data from PDFs and scanned financial statements.

Financial Spreading

Normalization of unstructured financial statements into analyzable formats.

Predictive Insights

Pre-computed ratios and anomaly detection to assist human credit analysts.

Workflow Acceleration

Automated routing, validation, reminders, and compliance checkpoints.

End-to-End Lending Lifecycle

  1. Profiling: Client prospect created and relationship profiled.
  2. Structuring: Loan request created with facilities and securities.
  3. Generation: Term sheet automatically generated.
  4. Decisioning: Credit adjudication via deterministic logic.
  5. Signature: Letter of offer produced and routed for e-signature.
  6. Funding: Disbursement completed by operations.
  7. Monitoring: Post-disbursement alerts and annual reviews.

Competitive Advantage

Compared to conventional LOS platforms, Cognitive Engine delivers:

  • • Deployment: 6-8 weeks vs. 12 months.
  • • Logic: No-code configuration vs. developer dependency.
  • • Automation: Fully integrated document AI vs. manual entry.
  • • UX: Intuitive design requiring minimal training.
